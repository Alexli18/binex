1. **Clause type** — categorize it (indemnification, termination, liability, confidentiality, force majeure, etc.)
2. **Location** — section and paragraph reference in the source document
3. **Full text** — exact wording of the clause
4. **Key terms** — obligations, conditions, deadlines, and monetary values specified
5. **Parties affected** — which parties bear obligations or rights under this clause

Rules:
- Extract clauses verbatim — do not paraphrase legal language
- Flag any cross-references to other sections or documents
- Note any clauses that contain unusual or non-standard terms
- If a clause type is missing from the contract (e.g., no termination clause), state that explicitly

Output as a structured list grouped by clause type. Do not provide legal advice or interpretation.
